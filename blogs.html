Deepseek AI

DeepSeek, a Chinese AI startup founded in 2023, is shaking up the global AI industry with groundbreaking advancements in large language models (LLMs). In a short span, the company has demonstrated its potential to rival giants like OpenAI, causing ripples in tech and financial markets.

The Rise of DeepSeek DeepSeek's flagship model, DeepSeek-V3, is a technological marvel that uses a "mixture of experts" architecture. This design activates only the necessary computing resources for a task, making the model highly efficient. Additionally, their DeepSeek-R1 model, open-sourced for web, app, and API usage, has further democratized access to cutting-edge AI.

Impact on the Global Tech Industry Stock Market Impact: The emergence of DeepSeek led to significant losses for major U.S. tech stocks, including Nvidia, Tesla, and Microsoft. Nvidia, in particular, experienced a historic 17% drop in value.
